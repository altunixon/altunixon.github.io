Part 1

Sanjuan Kingdom’s capital, Cadiz.

There the open ocean schooner Gabriel had returned after finishing its long voyage that took one whole year. The ship hull that was tired from the long trip was rested there.

『The temperature and wind of this country are really kind.』

『Is that so? All the other countries are saying that this place is really hot though.』

『That’s unimaginable compared to our country.』

The man said that and smiled gently.

His pure white teeth stood out dazzlingly.

It was truly a healthy and jovial smile, but that wasn’t the reason why the surrounding was showering him with their gazes.

His skin was black as though he had ink lathered on him. He was a black person who didn’t exist in this Aurelia Continent.

『Even so, I never thought that you will come here personally.』

『I’m more shocked that you are able to speak the language of our country normally instead, Admiral Jose.』

『I learned it from the people of Mahieule Island.』

Jose threw a careless smile back at the man.

Jose was someone who would get really engrossed with the things that he liked that he would even neglect his main occupation for them, but it didn’t take him that long to master the language of a foreign country.

It was terrifying because this man actually had continued studying inside the ship even during the expedition to Trystovy.

The first ocean exploration ship that Sanjuan Kingdom organized, Nataniel had sailed for several months and discovered Mahieule Island. That served as the impetus that greatly developed the south seas’ exploration.

And then the ocean exploration group finally discovered another continent that boasted a land size around sixty percent of Aurelia Continent with distance around two months of voyage from Mahieule Island.

The continent’s name was Oustralis Continent.

It had tropical climate with its own unique culture. Especially at the field of magic, they greatly surpassed the standard of Aurelia Continent.

In contrast their sailing technique and military technique weren’t so different with here. Because of that the harbor fell into panic when Sanjuan Kingdom’s ocean exploration group arrived.

『Even so it was amazing how it was our port that your ship stopped at first.』

『Well, that too is thanks to the sea god’s guidance.』

『It will be truly an honor if it was really god who selected our country.』

『I think that it’s surely true you know? It’s unusual that a royalty suddenly appear to accompany the exploration group back even though they don’t even know whether they will be able to return alive from visiting an unknown country.』

『Are you saying that I’m not usual for a royalty?』

It seemed the man was slightly bothered by that. He scratched his hair that was as black as his skin awkwardly. Even so his gesture of scratching his shortly cut black hair unexpectedly looked good. This man could act dignified when he wanted.

Jose secretly wondered whether this was what people called the bearing of a royalty.

Even if this man looked friendly, bright, and casual, he was a prince of a country with different language and civilization.

『Well, at the very least if the royalty that I serve want to do the same thing, I’ll put all my effort to stop him.』

『That’s painful to hear.』

It seemed the man had really faced a heavy resistance about his decision to come here. He was noticeably grimacing.

Jose got a bad premonition seeing that expression.

『……Don’t tell me, you really come here without any permission?』

『No……that’s……the only one I couldn’t persuade was my mother and wife, so there is no problem!』

『There is no way that’s not a problem! You’re in a big predicament!』

『I’m reflecting. But, I don’t feel any regret.』

『I’m begging you, please don’t bring a problem from your country to here, your highness Faysal.』

『……Will you accompany me to apologize to them?』

『Absolutely not!』

『I thought that there will be meaning to be the first person ever to visit a new continent……』

『I won’t deny that it’s something really effective.』

In fact due to Faysal’s visit, it was almost decided that the point of contact for trade with Oustralis Continent would be with his homeland Rialhama Kingdom.

It was unknown whether he should be called as someone resolute or reckless. But certainly there was no denying that the result was worth the big risk.

『──I want to quickly meet him. This young man called Baldr who brought that compass to admiral.』

There was a reason why Faysal boarded the ship Gabriel even though his family tried to stop him.

Among the thirteen nations in Oustralis Continent, his country Rialhama Kingdom was a medium level country that ranked sixth, but in the recent years they were getting backed into a corner due to a dispute for the control of the sea against the third biggest country there called Burundi Kingdom.

Of course if they fought at the coastal waters then Faysal thought that his country still wouldn’t lose, but it was really difficult for them to maintain the sea traffic route with other countries due to this dispute.

However if they could have a trade going with Aurelia Continent that still hadn’t developed any sea route with Oustralis Continent, there would be little fear of anyone getting in the way, and they would definitely obtain immense profit.

They would also be able to build a great fleet to destroy Burundi Kingdom someday.

He had to get ahead of the other countries to secure a lot of trade with this new continent as quickly as possible.

It was evident that the other countries would catch up with his country when the sea route was developed one day, but the advantage of being the first one to secure the market was great.

The prosperity of his homeland would be certain. Although it wouldn’t be able to last for a century, it would at least last for thirty years. That was Faysal’s prediction.

『Even so……I never thought that something like that would be sold that expensively.』

『Haha, even I was half in doubt when I heard the story at first! It was something that hadn’t be invented in this continent after all.』

『It was only used as dyes in our country though……』

『Right now it’s still something cheap, but I believe that in the future it will be as valuable as gold.』

Jose declared with a tone that didn’t sound joking at all.

However it was Faysal who was half in doubt instead.

『I don’t really get it. Just what kind of use the saltpeter from our country has for you?』

『That too is an invention of his highness Baldr you see……』

The trade with Rialhama Kingdom was a godsend that both Jose and Baldr never even dreamed of.

With this they would definitely be able to stand in the superior position against the church’s holy relics.

Especially because Jose thought that the flamethrower was unable to oppose the enemy’s rocket arrow. So this development was a lifesaver for him.

It might be possible to produce artificial saltpeter with Answerer Kingdom’s great national strength, even so they should be unable to reduce the cost and time needed.

『──Even so』

Faysal slowly looked around him with a very faint bitterness seeping through.

Cadiz’s harbor was an international trade harbor. It was usual for foreigners to visit here.

But even the citizens of such Cadiz couldn’t stop themselves from looking at a black person with gaze of curiosity.

Faysal was used to be showered with attention as a royalty, but that attention usually came in the form of respectful or calculating gazes. He was never getting looked at with curious gaze as though he was a rare animal.

Even if wasn’t to a degree that was especially unpleasant, it was unexpectedly uncomfortable to be exposed to such attention.

『I will have to get used to this kind of gaze too.』

『It’s to my great regret that I have to say that there is no other people in this continent with the same skin color like your highness. But if I’m allowed to express my humble opinion, I believe you’re your highness’s presence surely won’t become that unusual in this continent one day.』

『I see, they won’t need to be particularly curious if it’s not something that rare huh.』

His presence would stop becoming something unusual──Jose was declaring his intent that the interaction of this continent with Oustralis Continent would deepen from here on. It seemed that he was able to obtain quite some amount of trust as a business partner.

『To achieve that too, I’ll have to ask your highness to be acquainted with not only our country, but also with the leaders of various other countries.』

『I could ask for nothing better.』

However Faysal had a question in his heart.

If it was him, he wouldn’t do anything like handing over a source of trade like this to other country.

He would attempt to keep the secret and delay the leak of information as long as possible.

In the end what was Sanjuan Kingdom seeking from him?

Didn’t Sanjuan Kingdom consider that he might choose another country as trade partner?

His concern was completely mistaken.

First, Jose spoke about meeting the leaders of other countries, but he didn’t plan to include countries that were hostile toward Sanjuan Kingdom in that list.

In short Jose was planning to make use of Faysal’s existence to display to other countries that Sanjuan Kingdom had a relationship with this kind of country, and also to sell favor to them by acting as mediator, bringing two-fold profit for Sanjuan Kingdom.

Besides trade with Oustralis Continent was a special privilege that was only given to country in possession of ocean voyage technology.

Currently only Sanjuan Kingdom and Majorca Kingdom were in the possession of that. In the near future only Trystovy Kingdom would be able to join their rank.

There would be no problem no matter what kind of lip service Faysal gave to other country.

But there was a political problem that was more important than that.

(──Haha, you are really the only one who I don’t want to turn into enemy, your highness Baldr.)

Baldr’s idea was truly astonishing.

Baldr intended to spread the information about Faysal’s existence as much as possible throughout the continent. With that he intended to make all the people perceive the beatsman race not as outsider relatively to this newcomer, but as fellow people who were living in the same continent.

A visitor with black skin, different language, and different custom. Furthermore their population was incomparably bigger than the beastman race.

In contrast, although beastman race had slightly different form than human, they were still speaking using the same language and they were undoubtedly companions who had lived for many years in the same continent with them.

Who between the two they should be on their guard against as outsider element? Anyone with normal senses would be able to give the answer right away.

No matter how much Europa Church proclaimed that the beastman race was an outsider element that wasn’t blessed by god, there was this new foreigner that never even heard about Europa religion, and in the first place it was unknown whether they even had religion or not. How would they respond to this new factor?

Furthermore, there was also the addition of interests from trade with foreign cultural sphere that would complicate the matter further.

It was certain that the people’s prejudice and evaluation toward the beastman race would be thrown into chaos with this.

Jose felt fear from the bottom of his heart toward Baldr who managed to draw that chain of plans from the instant he received the report that Gabriel returned home with a black skinned visitor on board.

He absolutely didn’t want to have such person as enemy.

『──Right right, a banquet to welcome your highness’s arrival will be held tonight, so please look forward to it. Thanks to your highness, his majesty’s health recently is also getting better.』

『I’m happy to be of help.』

What managed to make King Carlos recovered from his temporary critical state was the medicinal herb that was brought from Faysal’s Rialhama Kingdom to Mahieule Island──it was something like a traditional Chinese herbal medicine that improved the immune strength.

Sometimes there would be rare case like this, where a patient who had been diagnosed as beyond help miraculously recovering due to this kind of alternative medicine. Carlos was exactly an example of such case.

Unfortunately it wasn’t an almighty medicine, but it was more than enough that it was effective for some people. That was all the more true if a king of the country was included in that group.

It was unknown whether the person himself understood how momentous it was, but for Faysal that fact had the worth of thousands of gold, both for his country’s profit and for advertisement.

(Someday, I’ll also go to his Rialhama Kingdom……)

Jose imagined the foreign southern country that he hadn’t seen before in his mind.

In order to enjoy such freedom too, first he had to subdue the wars in this continent.

Part 2

They would be tied with a firm bond with Baldr.

The two maidens praised each other’s effort that brought their feeling to be rewarded at this moment after risking even their life.

They needed more than one hour until their emotional tears stopped falling since they read the shocking marriage proposal.

The two rubbed their eyes that had swelled up completely and brew a pot of tea for a change of mood. They threw their back heavily on the sofa and their gaze wandered through the empty air for a while.

「It really feels like a dream.」

「Yeah……but, this ain’t a dream. We are gonna become bride.」

They were immersing themselves in a feeling of achievement that couldn’t be described with words, but they finally remembered that there was still a continuation to the letter.

「What what……?」

After the passionate proposal and words of love, there was the reality of holding a marriage ceremony waiting for them.

Even if they wanted to hold a marriage ceremony, it was unknown when the enemy would make a nuisance of themselves. There were also a mountain of other problems, like how they had to persuade Rachel’s father Welkin to attend the wedding.

And the biggest problem among them all was that they couldn’t allow Answerer Kingdom and the church to start the war until the marriage ceremony was finished.

「Fufufufufufufufufufufufu」

「Ahahahahahahahahahahahaha」

There was already no chain to stop the two maidens before the supreme bait that was the wedding.

Selina and Rachel laughed with eyes that were containing madness somewhere in them.

「In that case we gotta make them think twice about becoming a nuisance no matter what we gotta do.」

「There isn’t any need to hold back at all toward Baldr-sama’s enemy.」

Selina had inherited Masaharu’s cheat knowledge and way of thinking to some degree from Baldr himself.

Rachel too was already starting to learn the way Selina thought by working together with her.

Even if they were unable to hold a sword with their hand, they certainly had a way to fight back. And that method was hiding a sharpness and lethality that might even be worse than sword in some cases.

Perhaps it would be impossible to obtain those products anymore if their technology became lost.

Although he didn’t have the slightest intention of yielding the cutting edge technology in Dowding Group’s possession to other company after the war.

This production output caused oversupply of raw thread in Answerer Kingdom and made the price crashed down heavily.

Of course it wasn’t just string that was mass produced in vast amount. The foot operated weaving machines that were using cotton yarn were also in operation.

In addition, jacquard weaving machine that was using punch card for the first time in the world was also in the middle of development right now.

The moment the production was expanded using all those things, the industrial structure of this continent would surely be forced to go through revolution from its foundation.

There was a good example at earth with the establishment of England’s Magna Carta.

The way the attackers calmly and emotionlessly killed everyone with the thoroughness of a professional was enough to make Neuzer give up on his survival.

Their luck had run out the moment an opponent of this level had their eye on them.

But why? Why was an average caravan like them got targeted like this? Their cargo might be precious for the general public, but its worth for the military was extremely insignificant.

Even if their cargo was exchanged into money, the amount would only be enough for several days of extravagant spree.

A pro of this level shouldn’t target something like that. They certainly had another objective than the cargo.

「…………Ha?」

For a brief instant, Neuzer noticed that one of the attackers had a tattoo of blue moon. It was a total coincidence that he managed to catch sight of it.

「The church huh.」

He didn’t know what kind of circumstance there was behind the scene, but it seemed they had gotten dragged into the self-centered plot of the church and the kingdom.

It was infuriating, but he also felt resigned. No matter how hard he struggled, there was no way a group of this level would allow even a single cat to get away.

「Don’t screw with me, goddamn idiot!」

His legs were shaking fiercely from terror.

The massacre of the caravan was going to finish in less than a minute by the effective leadership of the assassin group.

Even Neuzer already had several soldiers moving toward him silently.

It was unreasonable, but death was approaching without any way for him to resist.

Neuzer suddenly broke out running toward a horse carriage.

He knew that the horse carriages had also been seized.

However Neuzer climbed up the horse carriage like a man possessed and reached out toward the flag of Dowding Company that was given to their company to show that their company was transporting Dowding Company’s product.

「Gahah!」

Two arrows pierced him from his back. Neuzer leaned on the flag and vomited blood.

「……Screw you!」

At the moment of his death, Neuzer believed that he had landed a blow back to their attackers and he fell into eternal sleep.

「Bury the corpses quickly! After that search for their change of clothes and change into them! We are going to be Hewrick Company’s caravan from now on after all!」

The assassin group that was composed of the church’s skilled agents. They were led by someone named Bezel. His subordinate reported to him.

「Dowding Company’s flag got dirtied by blood splash just now……」

「There should be a reserve flag just in case. Search it!」

「Understood!」

The thirty corpses were tossed into a hole that was one meter deep. The clothes and tents that were dirtied by blood were burned to ash. After the assassins finished disposing the proof, they began traveling to the next inn town smilingly like a normal merchant caravan.

One of the carriages was putting up a slightly old looking flag of Dowding Company with different color than normal.
