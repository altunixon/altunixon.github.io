Chapter 2 – An Extraordinary First Battle

.

Part 1

「……They are really careful even though they are only facing a viscount of a remote country.」

Baldr who heard the report of Haurelia army’s battle formation from his scout couldn’t help but let out a heavy sigh.

He was unable to cut corners with Antrim’s defense because of this territory’s geopolitical destiny.

It would be an abandonment of duty as a statesman if he knew there was a danger yet he ignored it.

However the opponent would also on their guard and increased their force the more he hardened his territory’s defense. It was a dilemma that Baldr couldn’t solve.

He believed that he was able to protect the most important military secret just barely, but it was impossible to completely hide the general detail.

That was only natural after he carried out a large public works that hired thousands of workers.

「It’s said that danger and good opportunity are the opposite sides of the same coin. If their serious invasion gets foiled, then even Haurelia might give up quickly.」

「Such possibility certainly exist.」

Baldr nodded at the words of someone familiar who he had known since he was a little kid.

He had a body built of a stereotypical front line warrior with his wide girth and muscles covering his whole body that he looked stout.

He was trusted by Baldr’s father Ignis and there was also nothing to criticize about his many years of real battle experiences. His sharp gaze and composed bearing was enough to grant a sense of security to the youngsters like Baldr or Brooks who were lacking experience.

His name was Cyril──he was Seyruun’s father, a knight who had served Cornelius House for generations and also Baldr’s future father-in-law.

He should be a personnel that was essential for Cornelius territory’s defense, but it seemed he was dispatched in a hurry to Antrim based on nothing but Maggot’s instinct.

Because there wasn’t enough time to mobilize an army, he rushed here with only less than ten subordinates while spurring their horses fast. They arrived here just the other day.

.

「Thank you for coming……but for some reason I cannot honestly feel happy about it.」

Baldr lamented like that when Agatha told him of Cyril’s arrival.

He was someone that Baldr desperately wanted in his force, but this also meant that Maggot had predicted that Antrim would become the center of the war.

When it came to war, Baldr couldn’t bring himself to doubt his mother’s instinct. He couldn’t help but feel slightly complicated.

「──Anyway, first we need to win a battle. I’m counting on you, Cyril.」

「Leave it to me. Aa, it’s also fine to call me father starting from now just in case the worst happen isn’t it?」

「NO WAY I’LL CALL YOU THAT RIGHT NOWWWWW!」

It seemed that even Cyril who was the very picture of stern and honest personality couldn’t keep his calm when it came to his daughter’s marriage.

「That’s too bad. Then I can’t allow myself to die no matter what until the wedding day.」

Cyril said that with a grin that showed his teeth.

Baldr finally noticed that Cyril was helping him relaxed as his elder. He scratched his head awkwardly.

He could also clearly feel that Brooks and Nelson and the others had let go of their tension after the exchange just now.

They too were nervous without realizing it themselves in front of the war that they would experience for the first time in their life.

「Sorry, you needed to take care of us like this.」

「No, your average nobles will have run away already since a long time ago. Our opponent right now isn’t an enemy that we can win against by fighting them head on.」

If Baldr planned to fight Haurelia army head-on, Cyril intended to recommend Baldr to escape even if he had to go against Baldr’s will.

It wasn’t like Ignis or Maggot had gave him that kind of order directly, but he was able to guarantee that those two didn’t have any intention to allow their son to die a dog’s death.

For Cyril, Baldr was his daughter’s husband, at the same time he was also the important heir of the house he served.

Although he also already understood from the start that Baldr fighting his enemy head-on was something that had no chance of happening.

.

「General Flandre who is famous as a general who will take the field himself, three knight orders, and then further two brigades of militias. Their number in total is about 20000. Certainly, if we fight them head-on I have the self-confidence that we will be annihilated in five minutes.」

When he spoke it out loud like this, Baldr spontaneously laughed at how great the difference in battle strength between his side and the enemy.

Normally a difference of number that was more than ten times wasn’t something that could be overturned. The best that could be done is such situation was to hole up in a castle with strong defense.

In order to fight and win──or making the enemy gave up at the very least, they would need to inflict a damage that far surpassed the enemy’s expectation.

He had prepared the way to do that. What was left now was only to believe in that and fought.

What they needed the most right now was a conviction of certain victory that could conquer their anxiety. After all since time immemorial, there was no precedent of soldiers who felt like they couldn’t win ended up winning.

「Now then, let’s start a dishonest battle.」

The ghastly battle that would be called as 「Antrim’s Tragedy」 by future generation was going to open its curtain.

.

Part 2

As expected, the first one who reported the approach of Haurelia army was the veteran mercenary Cell.

Other Antrim soldiers also went out as scout, but it seemed they weren’t a match against Cell who had instinct, experience, and body strengthening to improve his sight.

「……What’s more, for some reason they are placing their militias as the vanguard.」

Cell’s words allowed Baldr to confirm that Antrim’s defense had been seen through to some degree.

For the knight order that specialized in cavalry charge, Antrim’s defensive encampment had fatally bad compatibility with them. He should consider that the enemy had learned this.

「The vanguard is around 14000 troops that are centered around the militias that are split into two brigades. In addition, one knight order is sticking close to them as though to hide among the militias.」

Baldr guessed that unit was most likely the main force for the actual attack.

Actually this knight order was the Borodino’s Blue Dragon Knight Order. They had also borrowed magicians from the other knight orders and the number of their magician was twice the normal quota.

「Has the bridges at the south gotten burned?」

「All of them had been destroyed.」

The one who answered Baldr’s question was Nelson who returned back just now.

He led 200 soldiers with the mission to scout and intercept the combined army of Count Savoie and other nobles that invaded from the south.

Due to his mission where he had to accomplish holding back the enemy with just 200 soldiers, he had no choice but to destroy all the bridges along the border that could easily become invasion route.

Potomac River wasn’t a bit river, but it was a river with quite a lot of water volume. It was thought as impossible for soldiers to cross the river while wearing heavy equipment.

Currently Nelson was entrusted with almost all the cavalries of Antrim.

He already lost his relaxed attitude toward Baldr as his past schoolmate. Now they interacted with each other like a true commander and subordinate in battlefield.

「Send out scouts and take care to not miss them. Be careful so that they don’t learn our number before the battle.」

「By your will.」

Nelson bowed his head toward Baldr with a fearless smile, then he turned around and left the room to head where his unit was waiting.

「Now then, guess we will also go to meet the enemies.」

「They will surely cry in gratitude.」

Baldr’s eyes widened slightly at Brooks’s rare sarcasm and he laughed.

This guy, he is calmer than even me huh…….

「──I don’t understand anything difficult. I’ll just fight till the very end as a knight. I’ll leave the complicated things to you.」

「Really carefree aren’t you, this damn meathead!」

The two of them laughed loudly together.

A retainer who could trust his lord from the bottom of his heart without any hesitation, and a lord who worked out the plan with the full support of his retainer. That was how these two were acting.

「I have no intention to entrust my fate to god’s will. But, I have also already done everything I can as a mere human. I guess the only thing left to do now is to believe.」

「I always believe in you yeah, my lord.」

It was useless to worry at this late hour. The only thing left to do was to believe in their own strength and fight to the end.

The two of them felt refreshed after hardening their resolve and started walking with a manly expression.

When they exited the camp, around 800 regular soldiers of Antrim that Cyril had gathered were impatiently waiting for their order.

Among them, there were only around a hundred people who had been permanently stationed in Antrim since the beginning.

After all with the economic strength of the past Antrim, it was impossible to maintain the military force here except for a few hundred soldiers.

Half of the rest of them were soldiers that Baldr gathered from other territories by using money lavishly, while the remaining half were the children of Antrim territory’s peasant or merchant who voluntarily applied to become soldier after seeing that hope was finally starting to appear in their birthplace and they felt that they had to protect it.

Their birthplace was precious for them, like a Shangri-la of their soul that they didn’t wish for anyone to trample as they pleased.

However that birthplace of theirs was poor. It was bordered with an enemy country that had invaded many times to burn their house and field while pointing spear to their women and children.

Even when they were finally able to obtain a normal livelihood back, they were always living in suspense of when the enemy would come again to unreasonably plunder everything.

──However now was different.

Since Baldr arrived, Antrim was accomplishing a remarkable growth.

Many new residents were coming, there was no lack of work, their salary also increased, and even the products lined up at the stores were increasing in variety.

Depending on the situation, there were even some people who could save up money and became able to have an achievable dream like going to the capital and achieving success there.

Seeing their family harboring such dream, it was only natural that some people decided to offer their life in order to protect that.

There was no way the morale of soldiers who had something precious to protect would be low.

What Baldr obtained was most likely a troops with the highest morale in Mauricia Kingdom.

「──Soldiers, his excellency the viscount is present! Attention!」

With a single order from Cyril, the soldiers’ gazes were gathered on Baldr with smooth movements.

Their perfectly coordinated conduct was also the proof of how much training they had endured until now.

The height of the soldiers’ proficiency even shocked Cyril who had been in charge for many years of Cornelius’s troops that were famous for their strength. This result could be brought about only because of the height of Antrim’s morale.

Their everything was for the sake of their family and for the lord who brought future for their family──.

「Gentlemen, Haurelia army will soon arrive here. Their number is 20000, more than ten times our number.

The number 20000 that Baldr spoke caused groans and agitations to spread among the troops.

Although they were resolved, hearing the concrete number gave them a cold dose of how cruel the reality once more.

「In contrast our army numbered 1300. However there is nothing to fear. We will be the one who win.」

Yes, winning wouldn’t be difficult by all means.

「There is no way we will lose for the sake of the people who we should protect. Close your eyes right now and imagine the existence that you wish to protect. What is it that you have to protect no matter what? We exist here for their sake.」

The soldiers all nodded together by chance. After all they took spear in hand exactly for the sake of that.

「Surely the enemies also have their own family. They have their own comrades. They have their own lord that they should devote themselves to. However they are the invaders who want to plunder Antrim. That is the single unshakable fact. They have to be defeated no matter what. They have to be killed no matter what. This is the inevitable retribution for them.」

For human to kill other human, they needed determination in no small amount.

No matter how high their morale and capability were, half of Antrim army were new recruits who had no experience of killing human.

It was absolutely necessary to unshackle their heart.

「Don’t forget. The futures of many people are hanging on the tip of your spears. I order all of you gentlemen as the lord who is entrusted with Antrim. Kill without hesitation. Kill the enemies like killing harmful beast that attack the livestock. Kill them like an annoying fly that buzz around you at the summer. I will shoulder all responsibility as the one who gave all of you the order.」

In a proper battle, in a normal war that used spear and magic to fight, the casualties percentage wouldn’t be that high.

Even at the Warring States period where Sanai lived in, half of the dead were produced at the stage when the victor had been decided and the defeated side was pursued. The death percentage from the battle itself was only several percent.

But this battle would be different.

Baldr and his staff officers were aware that this battle would undoubtedly become a scene of massive massacre that had never happened in the history of this continent.

「Don’t leave your name behind as a dead hero. Kill your enemy in order to keep living vulgarly for the sake of your beloved one. I believe that all of you gentlemen are hero no matter what anyone else say.」

History might write of Antrim army in dishonor as genocider.

However Baldr should be the one who shouldered that stigma. He swore it.

Such stigma shouldn’t be shouldered by the militias who took up weapon for the sake of protecting their home.

「Now is the time for us to fire the beacon of war. All of you loyal and brave soldiers, hold your head high. If you surrender yourself to war for just this moment──it actually won’t really feel that unpleasant.」

In the battlefield, the price of life was like the price of apples in the market that got discounted because they were starting to rot. What was the worth of sanity in such place?

It was those soldiers who silently turned mad while still keeping possession of their composed judgment that was the strongest existence in the battlefield.

Sanai was aware of that from his experience.

In the first place, feeling that there was worth of living in the situation of killed and getting killed and even feeling pride from it wasn’t something that could exist from a normal situation.

「Soldiers, repeat after me!」

「Soldiers, repeat after me!」

「From now on we shall give our whole being to war!」

「From now on we shall give our whole being to war!」

The soldiers loudly recited Cyril’s words with earth-splitting volume. The agitation and fear from before had gone.

War didn’t only contain fear and despair, it also had terribly alluring longing toward life and victory. That was exactly why human couldn’t distant themselves from war from time eternal.

The karma of human was very foolish……and yet very beautiful.

Battle was terrifyingly cruel and mercilessly atrocious, and yet it was──fun.

「Very good. We are a small boat going down the raging stream of war. Surrender shall not be allowed anymore until we arrive at the ocean.」

「Victory for Antrim!」

The soldiers yelled in delight without Cyril even needing to urge them.

「Victory for Antrim!!」

.

Part 3

The defensive encampment of Antrim that came into their view looked even more troublesome than they imagined.

Haurelia army was in an overwhelming advantage when it came to number, but the encampment had been ingeniously constructed so that the battle fronts were extremely limited and they wouldn’t be able to make use of their advantage in number. There was no other word to describe this other than magnificent.

「It seems there is a master of battlefield construction among the enemies……」

Borodino along with his vice commander Maurice glared at Antrim’s encampment.

Even so there was no way they couldn’t stop with simply praising the enemy. After all Borodino volunteered to be at the vanguard for the sake of fighting and obtaining achievements.

「Calm and steady wins the race. We can only make use of our advantage in number.」

The number of magician under Borodino’s command was 800.

Borodino’s plan was to gather 70% of the whole army’s magicians to neutralize the enemy’s defensive encampment.

If the militias could destroy and break through the barbed wires under the cover fire of magic, they could let the power of number take care of the rest.

If there was something that was a concern, it was that an enemy who was able to construct such splendid defensive encampment couldn’t possibly neglect to prepare for such an occasion…….

「It’s useless to keep pondering like this. Let’s go, Maurice.」

「Yes, let’s go. Don’t worry, with this kind of clever trick, many times it had been proven that it’s actually not that useful in real battle.」

Certainly when a completely new tactic or an overly complicated tactic was proposed, often it would be proven that in real battle, such tactic was actually useless because they were unable to deal with the unexpected situations that cropped up.

But Borodino couldn’t imagine that such thing would apply to the enemy before their eyes.

.

Part 4

「Troops, advance!」

The large army was covering the area like fog. They advanced through Kareem Plain at the eastern part of Antrim.

The two militia brigades numbered around 14000. It was a huge army that the people who were born and raised at the countryside of Antrim couldn’t even imagine.

「Prepare to fire magic support──fire-!」

Countless fire balls were launched at the same time with the given order.

「Magic Cancel!」

Magic cancels were fired from Antrim’s encampment, but in front of the overwhelming number of magic from Haurelia, they could only erase around 30% of the fire balls.

Before long the fire balls landed with loud explosion. Cloud of dust and heat wave rained down on the soldiers who curled down at Antrim’s encampment.

「HOOOOOOTT! IT’S HOOOOTT–!」

Carlyle who became 18 years old this year and came from the southern part of Antrim was accidentally showered with sparks that flew at his neck. He screamed in agony.

「Brat, calm down. Your life won’t be in danger if you duck and put a coat on there to put out the fire.」

A veteran soldier who was in the same trench, Salil Baren barked at Carlyle and held down his head. He would only become a nice target if he carelessly stood up.

「Magic’s firing line is mostly a straight line, so they won’t hit as long as you stay inside here. Calm down and prepare. The enemies will come once the first wave of the covering fire has finished.」

Salil’s voice sounded no different at all than how he usually was. That composure made Carlyle forgot his pain and looked at Salil with respect.

There were several times in a man’s life where he had to display his courage, but there were few men who could display their courage when death was near like this.

「Salil-san, you’re amazing.」

「Even you will get used to it after going through this kind of thing several times. But in order to achieve that, first you have to survive no matter how unseemly it is. But kill the enemies as many as possible. If not it will be our allies who die in their place.」

「Yes-!」

Carlyle obediently assented with a smile. Salil felt an invisible poison permeating his lung after seeing such innocence.

(I hope I can keep this brat alive somehow……)

Salil had heard that Carlyle had parents and two big sisters. One of those sisters would marry soon.

This young man was filled with a pure sense of duty to protect his family and homeland.

He was a stereotypical naïve and loyal volunteer soldier, but Salil was familiar from experience that this kind of youth would be the one who died the earliest.

.

Haurelia’s militias started their work to remove the barbed wires and palisades. They were flummoxed by how difficult such work was.

Even though they could destroy the palisades, removing barbed wires without using specialized tools for it was extremely difficult. In addition they were in a battlefield, so it would only be natural that their work progressed very slowly.

In the first place the atmosphere of battlefield was unique. Few people had the mental strength to be able to continue doing removal work properly in such situation.

That was exactly the reason why specialized army branch like combat engineer existed, but the organization of Haurelia army was weak in such aspect. They were focused on using human wave tactic.

「Uguh……」

Another militia was taken down by a crossbow shot.

The damage to Haurelia army was enlarged due to attacks from the trenches. They were unable to make use of their advantage of number even now.

「What the hell-! The magic attack support isn’t useful at all!」

That’s right.

Antrim troops that were holing up in trenches or behind a protective wall were mostly unharmed even when they were under the magic attacks that were only fierce in appearance.

Most powerful magic had straightforward firing line. Magic with high-angle fire like mortar was still unknown. While magic like Explosion that could suppress wide area had short range, so they would need to get even closer if they wanted to use such magic.

「This is bad……」

Borodino didn’t expect that cover fire from magic would be this ineffective. His expression was clouded and he was grinding his teeth.

Of course he was convinced that his side’s victory was unshakable. If they could continue dismantling the defensive encampment like this, it would only be a problem of time until Antrim army collapsed.

However his pride as a commander couldn’t allow a situation where the damage to his troops was several times the enemy.

In the end the occupation of Antrim would be nothing more than a prelude in the war against Mauricia Kingdom. The war would become even harsher from here on, so they should take care to limit the damage to their side as much as possible.

「Stop the dismantling work for a moment and send the magician unit to the front. Be careful against surprise attack from the enemy.」

The magician unit started advancing with heavy armored infantries acting as their shield.

They advanced in order to use explosion magic that could rain down wind blast and heat wave from midair explosion to mow down Antrim troops that were holing up in the trenches.

「Tsk! Don’t let the magicians get near! Shoot! Shoot!」

Salil guessed the enemy’s plan and put his strength into his hand that was nocking the arrow.

Crossbow was a powerful weapon that could easily pierce armor, but it was unable to rapid fire.

In earth’s history, at the Battle of Agincourt, England compensated for this problem by using a lot of longbow, but Antrim army had nobody who could skillfully use longbow.

「Carlyle, don’t lose your head. First calm down and aim carefully. Just focus on not missing your shot.」

「Y-yeess!」

Carlyle was clearly at his wits’ end. Seeing him like that calmed down Salil instead.

「Aim at the legs of the soldiers with shield who are acting as wall. You can’t pierce their torso or head with arrow.」

「Understood!」

That’s right. He couldn’t allow this youngster to die.

If he could just calmly defeat the enemy with certainty without any other obstructive thought in his mind──that young feudal lord would think up a dirty trick to take care of this situation for them!

.

Inside the command post where it was possible to grasp the battlefront in its entirety──a pillbox that was protected by concrete, there was Brooks laughing fiercely.

「Of course you guys will come like that. After all you guys will win this unscathed if you can suppress the enemies one-sidedly using magician, so there is no way you won’t use this tactic.」

Brooks had seen through the intention of Haurelia army that wanted to lessen the risk to their troops as much as possible.

「The catapults are prepared to fire!」 (TN: Sorry to everyone, I mistranslated at the previous chapter. It should be catapults instead of ballista that are set up at the defensive encampment.)

「The target is the enemy’s magician unit, fire!」

Round balls were shot one after another in parabola trajectories.

To fire the balls, they used completely normal catapults that didn’t have any modification done to them. Originally they should be used to shoot rocks at a castle where enemies were holing up.

Haurelia army didn’t prepare any for the battle this time because they had advantage in number and prioritized the army’s advancing speed, but that wasn’t really an unusual choice.

「What are they thinking? What’s use is there in launching one or two rocks at this point……」

After Borodino said that,

The flying balls exploded with explosive sounds that tore the air.

It seemed the explosion wasn’t that powerful. The number of the death was few, but many of his important magicians were wounded.

「N-no good! Withdraw the magicians! Don’t let them harmed even more than this!」

Borodino gave the order in panic.

He couldn’t lose this trump card, the magician unit because of this kind of unknown attack.

Around the same time, Brooks who was watching the result of the attack also frowned because the damage to the enemy was less than expected.

「As expected, it’s difficult to set the timing with the fuse……」

To develop a fuse, a moderately modern manufacturing industry was necessary.

The best Baldr was able to do at present time was shooting high-explosive projectile made from gunpowder and fuse with catapult.

Because of that they couldn’t use this weapon perfectly because sometimes the explosive would explosive midair or even when it landed on the ground, often it wouldn’t explode.

「Even so──」

From the standpoint of Haurelia, they wouldn’t be able to avoid the situation from gradually getting worse with the magicians withdrawn.

Even though it was ineffective, the magician unit’s covering fire──their act of hindering the enemy troops using explosion and the like was also mentally supporting their ally. Such mental support effect couldn’t be underestimated.

Losing that was making the dismantling work to slow down even more.

Either way, Haurelia army being able to conveniently occupy Antrim in a short time with little damage was now nothing more than a pipe dream.

For Antrim army that wanted to buy time until reinforcement arrived, it would be greatly advantageous the more Haurelia army’s commander was reluctant to allow the damage to his army to accumulate further.

「What are you guys going to do next? I’ll see you guys off if you want to go home after this.」

Although he was saying that, Brooks was aware that Haurelia army wasn’t even considering retreat as an option.

.

「What was that explosion──?」

The great general Flandre who was watching the frontline from the rear reflexively stood up from his chair in surprise.

Fundamentally, Haurelia army was overwhelming Antrim army in quality and quantity of troops. He considered that the only problem was Antrim army’s defense.

Of course he also considered Antrim army attacking back as a form of defense, but it was impossible for him to consider that Antrim army would also attack using such powerful attack magic (at this point Flandre judged that the explosion was an attack magic from Antrim’s magicians).

「What happened just now might be the enemy playing their trump card.」

The commander of Black Dragon Knight Order, Lannes was monologuing while snorting in displeasure.

There was no change in his expression, but anyone who knew him would realize that Lannes was anxious right now that thing would become extremely troublesome.

In fact the number of casualties at the frontline had already reached a level that couldn’t be sneezed at. That sacrifice would become completely pointless if they temporarily withdrew here to rally themselves.

「──The enemy’s attack isn’t magic! It’s unknown what it actually is, but it came from a burning ball that was shot from catapult!」

The words from a messenger made Flandre hit the table in irritation.

Flandre felt that the situation had apparently turned into the worst possible situation.

If the attack wasn’t magic, that meant it couldn’t be cancelled using Magic Cancel.

Which meant they didn’t have the method to defend against that explosion.

In that case──.

「Send all the magicians from the knight orders of Black Dragon and White Dragon as well as the magicians from the whole camp to the frontline. Rain down magic attack on them without stopping. Don’t think of the consequence later, just don’t allow the enemies to raise up their head!」

Flandre discarded the thinking of saving his force for later.

In order to reduce the number of casualties, they should attack the enemies with their full force without thinking of the consequences later.

.

The intensity of magic attacks from magicians had increased even further.

Dust clouds were rising thickly. The return fire from Antrim army was also losing accuracy. Although Haurelia army was still receiving damage, they were managing to push back the frontline with certainty.

「Shit-! We can’t even raise our head like this!」

Salil cursed. He couldn’t even aim properly and fired his crossbow using only instinct.

Even so he managed to accurately capture the direction where there was enemy. As expected from a veteran soldier.

「How long this is going to continue……buwahh!」

Carlyle choked because a magic landed nearby when he opened his mouth and a lot of sand entered inside.

Salil stared at Carlyle in exasperation and laughed.

「I can forget how serious our situation is when looking at you brat.」

「Cough! Cough! You’re horrible, Salil-san……」

Salil received a reloaded crossbow from Carlyle and fired it with his instinct again.

Salil ended up firing Carlyle’s crossbow too right after it became impossible to raise their head and aimed.

Even if the inexperienced Carlyle fired at this situation, he wouldn’t be able to hit anything.

(This situation might be a bit dangerous……)

When the mysterious object that was fired from the catapult dealt a large blow at the enemy magicians, he thought that they might be able to push back the enemies at this rate, but it seemed it only made Haurelia army got serious instead.

Because of the intense support fire from Haurelia army’s magicians, the damage their side could deal to the enemy infantries at the frontline was reduced to half.

What’s more the other side won in number. It wouldn’t be strange even if the enemy made a move at the frontline soon.

Antrim army was barely able to protect the defensive encampment including the barbed wires, but if they lost that reliable protective wall, there was no doubt that they would be trampled under the enemy’s number.

「First battalion of Haurelia Kingdom army’s seventh brigade! Raise your speaaar! Thirty steps dash! Advance!」

Just as he thought.

Salil shook his head gloomily when he heard the enemy’s voice.

It seemed a part of Haurelia army succeeded in breaking through the defense line at the front.

.

「Looks like we win.」

Borodino talked to himself when he saw one of his subordinate squad succeeded in prying open the enemy’s defensive line.

Dismantling work was carried out behind the saturation attack that was rained down at the enemies without taking consideration of the mana consumption rate. Although their ally also received bigger damage than expected from it, it was still within the permissible range somewhat.

That defensive encampment couldn’t be underestimated, but it wasn’t like the enemies were holing up in a castle. It was impossible for them to overturn this difference in number. Borodino was thinking like that, but trench wasn’t something that lenient.

The attackers weren’t able to catch sight of the defenders was already a good enough defensive effect. And the risk of being stabbed from below when trying to climb over the retaining wall was very threatening in close quarter combat.

However, what Antrim army was counting on wasn’t the defensive effect of the trenches, they had another trump card.

「……Not yet. Lure them closer for ten more steps.」

Brooks was talking quietly toward his vice commander.

The vice commander returned a gaze of admiration at how composed Brooks was.

Since the last war, there were a lot of military personnel that hadn’t experienced a real military action. And yet even though Brooks was included in such category, his commanding ability really stood out. His command really didn’t seem like the command of a young man.

「This isn’t anything at all compared to the carnage that my best friend has survived. I want to be useful to that guy.」

His friend had become his lord right now, but the adoration that he had felt to Baldr since they first met had grown even stronger instead along with the passage of time.

That was especially the case after he witnessed the harsh training from Maggot that was completely devoid of sanity.

Unimaginable courage was necessary in order to stand up against an opponent that one knew was absolutely impossible to win against.

He wouldn’t have any face to meet with his best friend who was enduring far greater pressure than him if he was feeling scared from merely facing a battle where he had enough prospect of victory.

「Look! They are just cowards who don’t dare to show their face from their hole. Trample them and then return back in triumph with their head hanging from your spearhead! Chargeeee!」

Because Haurelia army was a huge army that numbered more than 10000, the chaos from water vapor explosion had temporarily robbed the function of their chain of command.

Zirco’s magically strengthened body rushed forward like an arrow that was fired from a bow that was drawn to its limit.

The soldiers that were standing along the line of Zirco’s dash got bisected into two like ragged clothes.

Zirco and the elite mercenaries were at their element when they tore at the defenseless rear of Haurelia army.

Water vapor explosion, burning oil, and then an ambush──it would be impossible for any army to maintain their organization in front of this combined terror.

「Is Viscount Antrim a demon……can you, can you really call something like this a battle-!?」

Flandre was enraged with a trembling voice.

Haurelia army’s defeat was already beyond doubt. He could even praise the enemy, well done.

However, he didn’t know about this kind of battle. No, he absolutely couldn’t accept this kind of battle as a warrior.

Generals and soldiers were just like rubbish in this battlefield, their skills and morale didn’t matter the least bit, everything was decided by the might of unidentifiable suspicious items. What kind of worth a warrior who had lived until now with a single spear had in such battlefield?

「Commander-in-chief, please retreat.」

Flandre was pulled back into reality by the low voice of a man who had hardened his resolve.

(Retreat──you say?)

He didn’t have any face to run away at this point. Flandre’s body trembled with new fury and he glared at the owner of the voice.

The commander of Black Dragon Knight Order, Lannes Bernard was standing upright there.

「You sir should be the one to gather the soldier and retreat. I intend to fight to the last as the rear guard.」

「──Even if that choice will lead Haurelia Kingdom to ruin?」

「What?」

Lannes’s calm words took Flandre completely by surprise.

「A mediocre warrior like me can’t possible explain what just happened. However this is the only thing that I understand. If that thing spread out through the whole Mauricia and our country is unable to deal with that, then there will only be ruin for Haurelia Kingdom.」

Flandre held his breath at Lannes’s tone that stabbed into his heart.

He didn’t want to admit it, he couldn’t possibly accept this kind of battle. Such feeling hadn’t changed but, what Lannes said was certainly true.

「I can’t possibly inform his majesty just what in the world has happened and what should we do to deal with it. General Flandre, you are the only one who can do that.」

Lannes was calm and composed even though he had resolved himself to fight to the death here. He was the embodiment of a true warrior that Haurelia could boast to the whole world.

Flandre gritted his teeth at his powerlessness that he had to sacrifice such person in this place.

「I won’t let your death to go to waste. I shall guarantee sir’s honor even if it cost me my life.」

「Those words are wasted on me.」

Lannes who always had a sullen face that made anyone wondered just what in the world was tormenting him was smiling brightly right now.

Flandre swore in his heart that he would never forget this clear smiling face for his whole life.

He also couldn’t die no matter what until he took revenge to that hateful Viscount Antrim.

After making such determination, Flandre immediately turned around.

「I’ll leave the Black Dragon Knight Order to you! We are retreating! Prioritize the reorganization of the militias!」

The majority of the knight order members had wounds somewhere on their body. However they were resolved to die and the height of their morale pierced the sky.

Sanai’s voice of admiration spontaneously leaked out from Baldr’s mouth seeing the splendid resolve of the rearguard.

A knight order with high loyalty──the knight order’s commander should be acting as the center foundation of that tight bond.

The commander was most likely a veteran knight with matured skill. As long as he could be defeated, even his subordinates who didn’t fear death would break down mentally. If their heart was broken, even soldiers who had resolved themselves to die would be unable to display strength that surpassed their limit. They would be reduced into mere soldiers.

Lannes was astonished by the harmony of Antrim army’s attack and Baldr’s capability in deciding to accurately charge right at him. He was feeling a mysterious sensation, as though he was facing a general who was even more veteran than himself.

Baldr’s spear handling turned fierce as though he had been switched with someone else. Lannes’s eyes widened in wonderment.
